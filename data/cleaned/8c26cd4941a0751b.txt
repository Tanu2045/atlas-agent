How long does it take to become job-ready for AI & ML roles in India?
The timeline varies based on your background. With a computer science degree, focused upskilling can take 6-12 months. For those from non-technical backgrounds, expect 1-2 years of dedicated learning. Many professionals start with entry-level data analyst roles and transition to more specialized AI positions as they build experience.
Are certifications worth the investment for AI & ML jobs in India?
Certifications can be valuable, especially for those early in their careers or transitioning from other fields. They signal commitment and baseline knowledge to employers. However, they should complement, not replace, practical project experience. Focus on certifications from recognized providers like Google, AWS, or Microsoft that align with your career goals.
