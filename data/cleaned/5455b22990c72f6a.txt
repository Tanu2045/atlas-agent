What is Generative AI?
Generative AI refers to AI models that create new content including text images, code music and videos Unlike traditional AI which analyzes data and makes decisions, Gen AI produces original content.
Popular Generative AI Tools
- ChatGPT & Bard – AI-powered text generation
- DALL·E & Midjourney – AI-generated images
- GitHub Copilot – AI-assisted coding
- Synthesia & RunwayML – AI-generated videos
Generative AI is revolutionizing education content creation software development and business automation making it one of the most sought-after skills for students
