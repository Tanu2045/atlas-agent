Top Generative AI Startups in India: 2024 Edition
India is emerging as a global hub for top generative AI startups. These top generative AI companies in India are making significant strides with their innovative applications. Here’s a look at some of the most promising Indian generative AI companies to watch in 2024.
Dubdub.ai
Dubdub.ai is a pioneering dubbing platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate video content in multiple languages. By leveraging AI/ML technology, Dubdub.ai reduces costs, speeds up processes, and ensures scalability and reliability. Their AI + Human-in-the-loop tool, Dubpro, enhances the synchronization of audio and visuals, making it a leading name among generative AI startups.
Listnr
Listnr empowers users to generate lifelike Text-to-Speech (TTS) audio using an AI Voice Generator, including top-tier AI voices. This technology is versatile and can be used for various purposes such as podcasts, audiobooks, and video sales letters. With over 900 voices in 142 languages, Listnr is a key player among Indian AI startups you need to keep an eye on.
CodeMate
CodeMate is an AI-programming assistant designed to boost developers' productivity. It automates the debugging process and provides recommendations based on industry standards. CodeMate is integrated with Visual Studio Code and can analyze code, making it one of the top artificial intelligence startups in India.
DhiWise
DhiWise is a smart development platform that converts designs into clean, modular code for web and mobile apps. By automating code generation, DhiWise streamlines software development, positioning itself prominently in the generative AI startups list.
Blend ai
Blend ai uses AI to automate ad campaigns for e-commerce companies. This platform simplifies online advertising and enhances marketing efforts, making Blend ai one of 2024’s top 10 generative AI startups to watch.
Neuropixel AI
Neuropixel AI focuses on the online fashion industry, using AI/ML and computer vision to create photorealistic synthetic models. Their technology automates apparel cataloging, making Neuropixel AI a standout in generative AI technology.
Alltius
Alltius provides AI-powered conversational assistance within software applications. Their GenAI assistants support users, consumers, and sales teams by offering personalized guidance, highlighting Alltius as a leader in generative AI applications.
Contlo
Contlo is an AI-powered marketing platform that executes personalized campaigns across multiple channels. By leveraging AI, Contlo automates customer experiences and optimizes marketing strategies, making it a notable entry among Indian generative AI companies.
These top generative AI startups in India are at the forefront of innovation, offering solutions that transform various industries. From dubbing and voice generation to programming assistance and marketing automation, these companies harness the power of AI to create impactful applications. Keep an eye on these startups as they continue to shape the future of technology in 2024.
