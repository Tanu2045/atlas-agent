India has emerged as a very favorable country for developing artificial intelligence solutions. Most of the startups in this article use Generative AI for practical issues. These Indian tech startups are developing relevant, innovative tools, products, and platforms that revolutionize the country's perception of technology. The following are 10 leading generative AI startups in India.
Sarvam AI was started by experienced ex-Amazon employees. It primarily laid down agendas to develop big language models across the Indian languages. Its core business is to make AI possible for everyone to use in their everyday lives. It has recently managed to obtain $41 million in a funding round.
Gan.ai uses generative artificial intelligence to personalize video messages. With this tool, brands can now record individual video messages for each customer. Gan.ai is being applied in several ways to enhance the effectiveness of companies' various marketing efforts.
With the support of Ola's cofounder, Bhavish Aggarwal, Krutrim is developing a massive language model for India. The organization intends to develop its Indian version of ChatGPT.
TrueFoundry is an AI innovation in India that accelerates the delivery of AI models for application developers. It has been referred to as the 'Heroku of AI.' It informs generative AI customer cases in the healthcare, finance, and e-commerce sectors. In 2023, it secured seed funding of $2.3 million.
Kissan AI is linked to farming since it consolidates relevant sectors. It employs generative AI to assist farmers with climate change, appropriate crops, and pest control. Its AI assistant is available in many regional languages. It is focused on helping small farmers adopt new technology.
Rephrase.ai is an AI writing program that enables the creation of videos from text and makes them realistic. These AI tools in India also offer brands a digital human creation service.
As an artificial intelligence company, Pixis transforms its focus to deliver specialized marketing solutions. Its primary tool is artificial intelligence, which generates campaign concepts, adverts, and data analyses.
Initially based in Africa, InstaDeep is expanding to India as well. It employs generative AI for logistics and supply chains.
Stage is an artificial intelligence app that features localized videos of stages of specific geographic areas. It enables writers to create content in the natural language varieties from the available generative tools.
WriteSonic offers AI-powered content writing. From blogs to ads, users can generate content in seconds. It's a top pick for marketers and small businesses in India.
