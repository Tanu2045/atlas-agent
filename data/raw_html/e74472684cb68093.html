/ Our Process /

Our Roadmap for the AI Development Process

Each project is unique, and we adjust our process accordingly to tailor seamless AI applications that adapt to your key business processes. Below is a glimpse of the main steps involved in building AI projects.

Define Your Needs

We analyze and enlist the problems you want to resolve by using AI or ML. This could include anything that you want to automate or enhance with AI.

Data Collection and Preprocessing

Collect relevant high-quality data that are focussed on your problems. We clean, preprocess, and format the data to ensure it is suitable for training the AI models.

Choose and Train the Model

We select a suitable machine learning algorithm or AI model that matches your problem. We train the model and fine-tune it to ensure excellent performance.

Validate and Evaluation

We divide the data into different sets for training and testing to validate the performance of the AI models and analyze it for accuracy with relevant metrics.

Deployment

Once the AI model is ready, we deploy it as a web or mobile app or add it to your existing systems. We ensure that the model is functioning as per expectation before deployment.

Monitor and Enhance

Post launch, our AI team continuously monitors the performance of the applications and collects data on user feedback, tracking key metrics for enhancing the performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build and launch AI solutions?

AI projects can be simple or complex, and depending upon many factors, it can take several weeks to months to build and launch them completely. The precise time depends upon the following:

  • Complexity level of the project
  • Data availability for training AI models
  • Exact business needs

How do you implement data security and privacy for my artificial intelligence project?

We are a responsible AI app development company and follow best practices to maximize data security and privacy for all projects. Some of the key strategies we implement for enhancing security in AI applications are:

  • Prioritize privacy by design
  • Encrypting data at rest and in transit
  • Regularly rotating encryption keys
  • Strict access control mechanisms
  • RBAC (role-based access control) mechanisms
  • Secure infrastructure
  • Compliance with industry regulations
  • Continuously monitor systems
  • Keeping our staff tuned to best practices
  • Follow secure development practices
  • Data Retention Policies

How much does it cost to develop an AI-based app?

Our consultants can provide you with an accurate, guilt-free estimate. AI development cost depends upon many factors, like

  • What features do you need in the app?
  • Project complexity
  • Type of business processes
  • The overall time frame for building the app

How can you track the progress of the project?

As a customer-focussed AI software development company, we adhere to agile principles and make it a point to regularly update the progress of the project to all the stakeholders. We also encourage you to use modern tracking tools like Basecamp, Zoho, etc. This will allow you to stay tuned with live-updates about the project.
