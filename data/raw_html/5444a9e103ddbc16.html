Frequently Asked Questions

I own an eCommerce business. Can you help me improve customer experience?

Yes, Generative AI can significantly enhance customer experience by generating personalized content, recommendations, and responses based on individual preferences and behavior. It can also create more engaging and interactive customer interactions through AI-driven chatbots and virtual assistants.

How can Generative AI be integrated into existing systems?

Generative AI can be integrated into existing systems through APIs, microservices, or direct embedding of AI models within applications. This process may involve working closely with your IT team to ensure seamless integration, scalability, and compatibility with your current infrastructure.

What is the cost of hiring Generative AI developers from Bytes Technolab?

The cost to hire Generative AI developers depends on the complexity of your use case, data requirements, and integration scope. For example, a basic GenAI chatbot using pre-trained models may start around $8,000–$12,000, while more advanced systems using proprietary datasets, custom APIs, or modular architecture can range higher. We offer both fixed-price and hourly models, and every engagement includes a detailed ROI analysis to ensure long-term value.

What deployment strategies do you recommend for Generative AI models?

We follow a flexible approach to the deployment of Generative AI, depending on your business model, infrastructure, and security priorities. Our strategies range from cloud-native deployment to on-prem setups, always backed by modular architecture and scalable infrastructure. We also incorporate MLOps pipelines for continuous integration and performance monitoring.

Do you offer consulting services for Generative AI adoption and strategy?

Yes, we offer tailored Generative AI consulting services to help businesses define use cases, assess feasibility, and map the right architecture. Whether it’s workflow optimization, data readiness, or ROI analysis, our consultants work closely with your leadership and tech teams to align AI investments with measurable business outcomes.

Do you offer custom Generative AI solutions or off-the-shelf products?

We specialize in custom Generative AI development built around your specific industry, use case, and proprietary datasets. Our focus is on delivering purpose-built solutions rather than off-the-shelf models—ensuring your IP, brand voice, and business workflows remain unique and competitive.

Are there risks during Generative AI migration, and how do you handle them?

Yes, legacy AI migration can come with risks like model drift, data loss, or performance degradation. We mitigate these by creating a safe staging environment, performing data mapping, validating models against test datasets, and applying rollback strategies—ensuring a seamless transition without impacting ongoing operations.

What is your development process for building Generative AI solutions?

Our GenAI development process follows 6 stages: discovery, data preparation, model selection or fine-tuning, iterative testing, scalable AI deployment, and post-launch monitoring. We build using modular architecture and ensure all models are integrated into robust MLOps pipelines for lifecycle management and long-term performance.
